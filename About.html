Throughout my undergraduate career, I have sought out varied work experiences from interning at a union for M.D.s in the New York City public hospitals to the Credit and Loans department at a community bank to Communications Assistant at a research center at MIT. In many of these positions, I had the opportunity to write and to analyze the efficiency and effectiveness of projects. I found that I really liked writing. I had the chance to do some advocacy writing and political writing with the unions I worked for and some science writing at the MIT Tata Center. I also dabbled in travel writing as an official blogger for my study abroad company. I used analytics to optimize audience engagement and the reach of organizational social media accounts and tracked effectiveness and best practices during a system-wide initiative in public hospitals to identify reproducible success strategies at any given location.

I’m enthusiastic about statistics, stories, and design and I plan to pursue a career at this dynamic intersection of disciplines. I’m interested in data journalism and information design. My relevant coursework includes Visual Journalism and Data Visualization Technologies. I like reading novels and news about outer space and I am currently looking for a full-time position in the DC metro area, where I am from. Go Nats!
